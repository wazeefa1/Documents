AAA RSA Partner App Guide

🚘 What is the AAA RSA Partner App?

The AAA RSA Partner App offers quick and easy roadside assistance services, connecting users to nearby service providers. Features include:

  • Real-time tracking of service provider location
  • Direct communication with the assigned partner
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden charges
  • Detailed service history

The app enhances roadside assistance with speed, convenience, and professional support.

🎯 Who is the App For?

Designed for partners providing roadside services, such as:

  • Towing service providers
  • Tire change/repair specialists
  • Battery jump-start technicians
  • Fuel delivery personnel

Partners can:

  • Receive real-time requests
  • Navigate with built-in or external maps
  • Update service status
  • Communicate with customers
  • Manage history and tasks

📥 Getting Started: Download & Installation

📱 For Android Users:

  • Open Google Play Store
  • Search "AAA RSA Partner"
  • Tap Install, then Open

🍎 For iOS Users:

  • Open App Store
  • Search "AAA RSA Partner"
  • Tap Get, then Open

💡 Ensure a stable internet connection and free space before downloading.

⚙️ System Requirements

📱 Android

  • OS: Android 6.0+
  • Storage: 100MB+
  • Permissions: Location, Notifications, Background Activity

🍎 iOS

  • OS: iOS 15+
  • Devices: iPhone 6s or newer
  • Permissions: Location (Always), Notifications, Background App Refresh

🧭 App Navigation

🧱 Main Screen Overview

  • ☰ Menu Icon – Open side menu
  • 🚗 App Logo – Brand identity
  • 🔔 Notification Bell – View new requests
  • 🟢 Online/Offline Toggle – Control request availability

📦 Task Cards

  • Completed Tasks – View history
  • Pending Tasks – Navigate, reach, confirm with PIN
  • Service Requests – Accept with vehicle number

📍 Bottom Navigation

  • 🏠 Home
  • 📞 Help
  • 👤 Profile

🚀 Navigation Flow

  • Tap bottom panel > “Click Here”
  • Select marker > “Show Route” > “Start Guidance”
  • Follow voice directions
  • Upon arrival: “Stop Guidance” > “Reached” > Confirm

⚙️ Settings & Permissions

  • Location: Always Allow / Allow All the Time
  • Notifications: Must be enabled
  • Battery Optimization (Android): Disable for the app

⚠️ Missing permissions may impact real-time tracking and functionality.
